Join me on unforgettable small-group excursions, each offering a distinct theme and immersive experience in captivating locations. Whether we're chasing sunsets on secluded beaches or capturing the serenity of snowy landscapes, every moment promises to be a memorable journey.

Explore South Korea

Immerse yourself in South Korea's rich cultural tapestry on our photography excursion from Seoul to Busan and Jeju. Capture the vibrant markets and serene temples of Seoul, the dynamic blend of urban modernity and coastal charm in Busan, and the breathtaking beauty of Jeju's volcanic landscapes. Explore each destination's unique cultural and natural landscapes, creating unforgettable memories through your lens.

Spanish Adventures

Join me for a seaside photography excursion along Spain's picturesque shores. Capture the beauty of coastal landscapes, from golden sunsets to rugged cliffs. Explore hidden coves and sandy beaches, capturing moments of tranquility and natural beauty.

Venture into the heart of the mountains on this photography excursion that celebrates alpine beauty.
